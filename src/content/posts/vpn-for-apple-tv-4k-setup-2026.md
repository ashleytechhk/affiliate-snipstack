## What Is a VPN for Apple TV 4K?

A VPN — Virtual Private Network — routes your internet traffic through a server in a different location. When streaming services see your connection, they see the server's location rather than your actual home address. That location change unlocks two things: regional content libraries and regional pricing.

Apple TV 4K runs tvOS, Apple's closed operating system. Unlike Android TV devices, tvOS does not support third-party VPN apps directly. That means you cannot install Surfshark the same way you would on an iPhone. Instead, there are three practical workarounds:

1. **Router-level VPN** — Install the VPN on your home router. Every device on your network, including Apple TV, inherits the VPN connection automatically.
2. **Smart DNS** — Some [VPN providers](https://snipstack.io/vpn-providers-best-2026/) offer a Smart DNS feature that reroutes only streaming traffic without full encryption. Surfshark includes this.
3. **Mac sharing** — Share a VPN connection from a Mac on the same network. Useful for testing but less stable for daily use.

For most households, the router method is the cleanest long-term solution.

**Step 2 — Log into your router admin panel.** Typically accessed at 192.168.1.1 or 192.168.0.1. Navigate to the VPN client section.

**Step 3 — Import the Surfshark config file.** Choose the server location you want. For streaming savings, pick the region where your target service offers lower pricing. Apply and save.

**Step 4 — Reboot your router.** Confirm the VPN is connected by checking your router's status page. The WAN IP should now reflect the VPN server's location.

**Step 5 — Test on Apple TV.** Go to Settings > Network on your Apple TV and note the IP address. Cross-reference it with a browser test to confirm the location shows correctly.

**Step 6 — Open your streaming app.** If you are accessing a new regional store, you may need to sign in with an account registered to that region, or create a new one.

### Option 2: Surfshark Smart DNS

Smart DNS is simpler but does not encrypt your traffic. It is best for households where streaming speed is the priority and privacy is less of a concern.

In your Surfshark account, find the Smart DNS section and note the two DNS server addresses provided. On your Apple TV, go to Settings > Network > Wi-Fi > Configure DNS > Manual, and enter those addresses. That is the entire setup. No router changes needed.

### Option 3: Mac Sharing

Connect your Mac to the VPN. Then go to System Settings > General > Sharing > Internet Sharing, share your Ethernet connection over Wi-Fi or vice versa. Connect Apple TV to that shared network. This works but adds a dependency on your Mac staying on.
