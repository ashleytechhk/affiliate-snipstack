## What Is a VPN and Why Does Canada Streaming Need One?

## How Canadian Streaming Geo-Blocking Works

Streaming platforms enforce geographic restrictions through IP-based detection. Every IP address is registered to a geographic region. When your IP resolves to Canada, platforms grant access to Canadian-licensed content. When it resolves to anywhere else, they check their licensing agreements — and for Canadian-exclusive services, the answer is access denied.
