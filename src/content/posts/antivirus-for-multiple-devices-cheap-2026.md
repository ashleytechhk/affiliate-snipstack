## What Happens When One Device Is Left Unprotected

You might assume that one unprotected device does not matter much if your main computer has antivirus installed. That assumption is wrong, and criminals know it.

Here is how the gap gets exploited in practice.

### The Phone Attack

A scam text message arrives on your phone — it looks like a delivery notification, a bank alert, or a message from a well-known company. You tap the link. Your phone has no web protection. A fake page loads, designed to look exactly like your bank's website. You enter your details. Within minutes, your account is compromised.

A grandchild uses the family tablet to download a free game or app from an unofficial source. Buried inside is a piece of software that records passwords. The next time you log into your email or bank on that tablet, your credentials are captured and sent to the attacker.

In each of these scenarios, the attack went through the weakest device in the home. [Real-time antivirus protection](https://snipstack.io/antivirus-with-safe-banking-browser-2026/) on every device removes those weak links at once.
