## Why Seniors Are the Number One Target

Cybercriminals are not random. They target people they believe will pay — and they have studied the numbers carefully.

## What Ransomware Actually Does to Your Computer

You open an email that looks like it is from your bank, or a shipping company, or even a friend. You click a link or open an attachment. Nothing seems to happen immediately.

But in the background, software has started running. Within minutes, it begins encrypting — scrambling — every file on your hard drive. Then a message appears on your screen demanding payment — often hundreds or thousands of dollars — in exchange for a code that will unscramble your files.

The reality is this: even if you pay, there is no guarantee you will get your files back. Law enforcement and cybersecurity experts consistently advise against paying ransoms. Yet many seniors do pay, because the thought of permanently losing decades of family photographs is unbearable.
