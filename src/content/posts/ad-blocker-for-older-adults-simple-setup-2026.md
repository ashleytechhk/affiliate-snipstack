## What Is an [Ad Blocker](https://snipstack.io/ad-blocker-that-blocks-cookie-consent-popups-2026/), Exactly?

An [ad blocker](https://snipstack.io/ad-blocker-for-android-without-root-access-2026/) is a tool that prevents advertisements from appearing on websites and apps. Instead of seeing a banner ad for a product you searched once three weeks ago, you see the actual content you came for.

There are two main types:

- **Browser extensions** — Add-ons you install inside a web browser like Chrome or Firefox. They work well on desktops but can be tricky on mobile.
- **Network-level blockers** — Tools built into an app (like a VPN) that filter out ads before they reach your device. These work across all apps and browsers simultaneously.

For older adults who want simple setup, network-level blockers win easily. You install one app, flip one switch, and every app on your phone benefits.
