## How These Apps Are Different (And Why It Matters)

Before diving into the comparison, it helps to understand that spam blocker apps on Android fall into two categories:

**Reactive blockers** — They screen or block calls after your number is already in the wild. Useful, but they're playing catch-up. Every call they block is proof your real number got shared somewhere it shouldn't have.

For general spam from unknown sources, reactive blockers are fine. For online dating — where you're deliberately handing your number to strangers — a proactive tool like Alt-Number does what blockers can't.

### 2. Google Call Screen — Best Free Built-In for Android

**Google's Call Screen intercepts unknown calls before your phone rings.** The caller hears a screening message and is asked to state why they're calling. Their response appears as a live transcript on your screen. You choose whether to answer, decline, or mark as spam.

For Pixel owners, this is completely free and built into the dialer. It handles robocalls and repeat telemarketers well. The limitation is what Call Screen cannot do: it only works on calls that already reach your number. It does nothing to protect the number from being shared or discovered.

For online daters, Call Screen is a reasonable second layer on your real number — but it shouldn't be your only protection. Combine it with Alt-Number and you've got both ends covered.

**Availability:** Pixel 3 and above; some third-party Android devices with Google Phone app. Not available on iOS.

### 3. Hiya — Best Third-Party Spam ID App for Android

**Hiya identifies spam, scam, and robocall numbers and flags or blocks them before they reach you.** The free tier covers caller ID and spam warnings. The paid tier adds automatic blocking and reverse number lookup.

Hiya integrates directly with your Android dialer, so there's no separate calling interface. Setup takes under two minutes. The database is extensive and updated frequently — it catches most high-volume spam numbers reliably.

The reverse number lookup feature is worth highlighting for online daters: if someone texts you from an unknown number claiming to be a match, you can check the number against Hiya's database before responding. Not foolproof, but a useful sanity check.

**Limitation:** Like all reactive blockers, Hiya only screens calls that hit your real number. It's defense, not prevention.

### 4. YouMail — Best for Smart Voicemail + Robocall Deflection

For online daters: YouMail shines as a cleanup tool if your real number has already been shared too widely. It won't prevent new exposures, but it does clean up the inbound noise.
