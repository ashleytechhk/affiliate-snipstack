## Why Legacy Suites Feel So Heavy

Norton and McAfee were built when "security software" meant catching viruses on Windows XP. Today's threats are different in kind, not just scale. Tracking pixels follow you across the web. Ad networks build behavioral profiles from your browsing. Unsecured connections on coffee shop Wi-Fi let anyone intercept unencrypted traffic.

A traditional antivirus scanner does nothing about any of that.

![How modern threats differ from legacy antivirus coverage](/og/cyber-security-solutions-2026-inline-1.png)

Both Norton 360 and McAfee Total Protection have tried to patch these gaps by bundling VPNs and identity tools into higher-tier plans. The results are mixed. Norton's VPN uses Aura's infrastructure and has no-logs claims — but the free tier caps you at 200 MB per day, which covers roughly one YouTube video. McAfee's Safe Connect VPN is similarly restricted unless you pay for their premium plan.

Neither company's core strength is VPN technology, and it shows.
