## Why TVNZ+ Is Geo-Locked

The block is not arbitrary. TVNZ+ acquires broadcast and on-demand rights to content on a territory-by-territory basis. A drama produced in the UK, a reality format licensed from the US, or a local commission with international co-production funding — each comes with rights that may only cover New Zealand. Allowing global access would breach those contracts and undercut the international distributors who paid for rights elsewhere.

The mechanism is simple: every connection to TVNZ+ triggers an IP-to-location lookup. A New Zealand IP passes. Anything else receives a geo-block error. Your account credentials are valid; your location is not.

This means the fix is equally direct — you need a New Zealand IP address. The practical wrinkle is that TVNZ+, like most major streaming platforms, also maintains blocklists of known VPN server addresses. The key is choosing a VPN that rotates its IP pool fast enough to stay off those lists.
