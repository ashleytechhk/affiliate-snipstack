## The Three Ways Your Real Number Becomes a Problem on Telegram

### 1. Contact Sync Exposes You Automatically

Telegram syncs with your phone's contact list. When you register with your real number, any Telegram user who already has that number saved sees your account appear in their contacts — automatically, without any action from you.

This means:
- A match you rejected on a dating app three months ago can see your Telegram profile if they saved your number
- If your number ended up in someone's contact list through a data broker or a previous leak, they now have a path to your Telegram identity
- Anyone who runs your number through a contact sync sees your display name, username, and profile photo

You have not chosen to connect with these people. Telegram's default settings made that connection for you.

### 2. Your Number Ties All Your Accounts Together

Most people use the same phone number across multiple services — bank alerts, delivery apps, work accounts, social media verification. When your Telegram registration number is real and known, it becomes a thread that ties all of those accounts together.

A bad actor who learns your Telegram registration number now has a useful piece of information: a number they can try across other platforms, run through data broker search tools, or use in a SIM swap attempt. The number itself becomes a vector.

### 3. Telegram Now Shares Phone Numbers With Authorities — and Telegram Itself Holds Them

In September 2024, Telegram updated its privacy policy to confirm it would hand over users' IP addresses and phone numbers to authorities who present valid legal requests. This is not a critique of the policy — law enforcement access exists on most major platforms. The relevant point is simpler: Telegram stores your real phone number, and that number is now formally part of the data picture associated with your account.

If your account is ever flagged, reported, or involved in any legal process — even incorrectly — your real number is in the record. For someone using Telegram specifically for dating privacy, that is worth knowing.
