## Why Iview Is Geo-Locked in the First Place

The block is contractual, not arbitrary. ABC acquires broadcast rights territory by territory, the same way every major platform does. A UK drama or co-produced series may only be licensed for Australian audiences, with separate distributors holding rights elsewhere — streaming it globally for free would undercut those deals.

The enforcement is straightforward: every request to iview triggers an IP-to-location check. An Australian IP passes through; anything else returns the geo-block message, regardless of your account or citizenship.

That single point of enforcement is also the single point of failure — exactly what a VPN uses, by presenting an Australian IP instead of your real one. The wrinkle: iview maintains blocklists of known VPN IPs, so the VPN you choose needs to rotate its addresses faster than those blocklists catch up.
