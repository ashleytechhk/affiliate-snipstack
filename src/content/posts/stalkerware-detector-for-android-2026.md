## What Is Stalkerware? (Plain English)

Stalkerware is a type of software that hides on your phone and secretly collects information about you. Once it is installed, it can:

- Read every text message you send and receive
- Listen through your phone's microphone
- Track your exact location using GPS
- See every website you visit
- Record passwords you type, including for your bank account

Unlike a normal app, stalkerware does not show up on your home screen. It runs in the background, invisible, sending all your private information to whoever installed it.

It is usually installed by someone who has had physical access to your phone — a family member, a carer, or sometimes a scammer who tricked you into installing something. The person being spied on almost never knows it is there.

## Warning Signs: Is Stalkerware Already on Your Phone?

You do not need to be a technology expert to spot these warning signs. Check if your Android phone is showing any of the following:

**Your battery drains much faster than usual.** Stalkerware runs constantly in the background, which uses extra battery power. If your phone suddenly needs charging twice a day when it used to last all day, take notice.

**Your phone gets warm even when you are not using it.** A phone that is sitting on the table but feels hot has something running that should not be.

**Your mobile data is being used when you are not online.** Stalkerware sends your information over the internet. If your data bill is higher than usual, hidden software may be the reason.

**The phone is slower than it used to be.** Background spying software takes up processing power, which makes everything else run more slowly.

**Strange apps appear that you did not install.** Even if they use confusing names — "System Manager," "Phone Updater," "Battery Booster" — apps you do not recognise deserve a close look.

**You notice your calls being cut off or your screen lighting up on its own.** These can be signs of software accessing your phone without your input.

If you recognise two or more of these signs, it is time to run a proper check.
