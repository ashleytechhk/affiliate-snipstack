Norton 360 remains the reference standard for antivirus detection. **Norton makes sense if:**

**Norton becomes harder to justify if:**
- You want to cover more than five devices without paying for a higher tier
- You find the upsell prompts and interface friction genuinely annoying
- The renewal price jump makes your effective annual cost $80–120 per year
