## Why Speed Matters Specifically for Subscription Arbitrage

Subscription arbitrage is the practice of using a VPN to sign up for streaming services through a lower-cost regional pricing tier. Netflix Turkey, YouTube Premium India, Spotify Argentina — in each case, the service is identical but priced for a local market.
