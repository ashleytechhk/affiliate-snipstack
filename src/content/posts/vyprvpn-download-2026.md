### 4. **VyprVPN Download for Android — Google Play and APK**

VyprVPN is available on the Google Play Store. The Android app is the most full-featured mobile version: all four protocols including Chameleon, proper split tunneling (you can route individual apps outside the tunnel), and a kill switch that prevents traffic leaking if the connection drops.

For Android users doing streaming arbitrage, the split tunneling feature is particularly useful. This reduces the chance of location-detection friction from services that check your GPS location against your IP location.

### How does VyprVPN's Chameleon protocol work?

Chameleon scrambles VPN packet metadata so that deep packet inspection systems cannot identify the traffic as VPN traffic. This makes it harder for networks to block VPN connections. It is particularly useful on networks that actively filter VPN protocols. For standard streaming arbitrage on residential broadband, Chameleon is generally not necessary — WireGuard is faster and sufficient.
