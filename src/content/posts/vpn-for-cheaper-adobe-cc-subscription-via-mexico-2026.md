## What Is a VPN, and What Is "Regional Pricing"?

A **VPN (Virtual Private Network)** routes your internet connection through a server in another location before it reaches the site you're visiting. The site sees the VPN server's IP address instead of yours, making you appear to be browsing from wherever that server sits. Connect to a server in Mexico City, and Adobe's website reads your connection as coming from Mexico.

**Regional pricing** is charging different amounts for the same product depending on the country a customer appears to be in, adjusted for local purchasing power and currency strength. Adobe, Netflix, Spotify, and most subscription software use some version of this model.

Put the two together: a VPN can make Adobe's website believe you're in Mexico, which is the step required to see — and potentially sign up at — Mexico's storefront pricing.

## The Catches You Need to Know About

This is the part most "how to save on Adobe CC" posts skip.

**Payment method is the real bottleneck.** Adobe generally expects billing that matches the account's country — a Mexican payment method or a card Adobe accepts for a Mexico-billed subscription. An international card sometimes works, sometimes doesn't, and this varies over time. This is usually where the plan breaks down, not the VPN itself.

**Existing subscribers face a harder path.** If you already hold an active US plan, you generally can't switch your account's country while subscribed — you'd need to cancel first, possibly losing any promotional pricing.

**Adobe's terms of service govern account country.** Using a VPN is legal in most places, but presenting a different apparent location to access region pricing may run against the letter of Adobe's terms. Enforcement here is typically account-level (correcting pricing, requesting verification) rather than anything more severe — but there's no guarantee your setup goes smoothly long-term.

None of this means the approach doesn't work — plenty of people complete Mexico-region signups successfully. The VPN gets you to the right pricing page; your account and payment details determine whether the signup goes through cleanly.
