## The Privacy Gap That Persists After You Delete the App

Deleting a dating app or closing a gig platform account does not delete the data that has already moved downstream. This surprises people who assume that account deletion equals data deletion.

The data broker that indexed your number months ago is not bound by the platform's privacy policy.
