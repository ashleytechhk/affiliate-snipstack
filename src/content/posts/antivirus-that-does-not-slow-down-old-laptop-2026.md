## What "Lightweight" Antivirus Actually Means

Not every antivirus works the same way. A lightweight tool checks only what needs checking and leaves the rest of the computer alone.

**Cloud-based scanning** instead of a giant local database means your laptop sends a small query to a server rather than storing millions of virus signatures on the hard drive.

**Scanning on demand**, not on a fixed schedule, means files are checked only when downloaded, opened, or installed — not the thousands already sitting untouched.

**No bundled extras** running in the background — no system "optimizers," toolbars, or constant pop-ups competing for your laptop's limited memory.
