## Troubleshooting: Common Problems and Simple Fixes

### My computer is running slowly after installation

Close any programs you are not using. If you just ran a full scan, the slowdown is temporary. Restart your computer and normal speed should return. If the problem continues, check that you do not have two real-time scanners running at the same time — for example, both Avast and Windows Defender scanning simultaneously can cause conflicts.

### I forgot the password I created

On the login screen, click **Forgot Password**. Enter your email address. Check your inbox (including your Spam or Junk folder) for a reset email and follow the link inside.

### A pop-up says my computer is infected — should I trust it?

Only trust warnings that come from inside the antivirus app you installed. Pop-ups that appear in your web browser — especially ones with a phone number to call — are almost always scams. Close the browser tab immediately, do not call any numbers, and do not enter any personal information. Run a scan from your antivirus app to confirm everything is fine.

### The installer will not run or gives an error

Try right-clicking the downloaded file and choosing **Run as Administrator**. If the problem continues, restart your computer and try again. Make sure your internet connection is active during installation as some installers need to download additional files.

### Windows Defender keeps turning itself off

This sometimes happens when a third-party antivirus is installed — Windows automatically steps back so both tools are not running simultaneously. This is normal behaviour. Your new antivirus has taken over protection duties.
