## Why RTVE Content Is Geo-Locked

RTVE licenses part of its catalogue by territory. Some programming is fully owned by RTVE and could technically play anywhere, but plenty of series, films, and sports segments are acquired or co-produced under agreements covering Spain only. Opening that content globally would breach those terms and undercut whoever holds distribution rights elsewhere.

The enforcement is an IP-to-location check that runs every time you load a stream. A Spanish IP passes; anything else triggers the block, regardless of whether your login is correct. The fix is narrow: you need a Spanish IP address. The wrinkle is that RTVE, like most platforms, maintains blocklists of known VPN server ranges — a VPN with a small, static IP pool gets caught quickly; one with active rotation stays usable longer.
