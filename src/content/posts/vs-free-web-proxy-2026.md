## What a Free Web Proxy Actually Does

A web proxy sits between your browser and a target website. You paste a URL into the proxy's interface, it fetches the page on your behalf, and reflects the result back to you. Your actual IP is hidden from the target site — at least in theory.

The key limitation is that it operates at the application layer for a single browser tab. It does not tunnel your whole connection. Video streaming relies on persistent sockets, adaptive bitrate streams, and session tokens that proxy services simply cannot handle cleanly. Most major streaming platforms have also built detection systems specifically targeting the IP blocks that free proxy providers use, because those same IPs get hammered by millions of users simultaneously.
