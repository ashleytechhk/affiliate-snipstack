## Platform Coverage: Important Gaps to Know

Neither product covers iOS with a full antivirus engine — Apple's App Store restrictions make true on-device scanning impossible on iPhone and iPad for any antivirus vendor. Both offer VPN or browser-based protection on iOS instead.

**Surfshark Antivirus** supports Windows 10+, macOS 11 (Big Sur) and later, and Android 10+ (64-bit only). If you are running Windows ARM or 32-bit Android, Surfshark Antivirus is not compatible.

**ESET HOME Security** supports Windows 10/11, macOS 13 and later, and Android. ESET's macOS requirement is higher — users on macOS 11 or 12 would need to stay on Surfshark or an older ESET version. ESET also offers separate products for Linux server environments, which Surfshark does not.

## Privacy and Company Background

**Surfshark** is headquartered in the Netherlands and operates under EU jurisdiction. Its no-logs VPN policy has been independently audited. The company is part of Nord Security (the same group behind NordVPN), which means shared infrastructure investment and scale — but some users prefer products from fully independent entities.

**ESET** is a Slovak company headquartered in Bratislava, operating independently. It has published transparency reports and has no documented history of data compliance issues. For users concerned about company jurisdiction, ESET's independent Slovak origin may be a plus; it sits outside Five Eyes and Fourteen Eyes intelligence-sharing arrangements.

Neither company raises serious red flags for consumer privacy. For users with elevated threat models, ESET's independent ownership and non-EU5-Eyes jurisdiction may be preferable. For everyone else, both are reasonable choices.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### [Is Surfshark](https://snipstack.io/is-surfshark-one-worth-the-upgrade-2026/) Antivirus as good as ESET at detecting malware?

ESET has a more established detection track record. It earned AV-Test "Top Product" status in February 2026 with consistently high scores across multiple testing rounds. Surfshark Antivirus is AV-Test certified with strong marks, but lacks ESET's Exploit Blocker and LiveGuard sandbox — tools designed to catch sophisticated, non-file-based attacks. For everyday consumer threats, both are effective. For demanding threat environments, ESET's detection stack goes deeper.

### [Does Surfshark](https://snipstack.io/does-surfshark-one-include-email-protection-2026/) Antivirus work without the VPN?

The antivirus component of [Surfshark One](https://snipstack.io/surfshark-one-vs-avast-one-review-2026/) functions independently — you do not need to activate the VPN to scan for malware. They are separate tools within the same subscription. You can run the [antivirus with](https://snipstack.io/antivirus-with-game-mode-no-interruptions-2026/)out routing any traffic through a VPN server.

### Can I get ESET with a VPN included?

Yes, but only on ESET HOME Security Premium, the top tier. ESET Essential and NOD32 do not include VPN access. ESET's bundled VPN is described as a basic privacy tool — it is not a full-feature VPN service with a global server network and streaming optimization. [Surfshark One's VPN](/blog/antivirus?sub=surfshark-antivirus-vs-eset-comparison-2026__faq) covers 100+ countries and is the same service used by dedicated VPN subscribers.

### How many devices does Surfshark Antivirus cover?

Surfshark Antivirus covers up to 5 devices per subscription. Note that this is the antivirus component specifically — the VPN included in [Surfshark One](https://snipstack.io/surfshark-one-vs-norton-360-comparison-2026/) supports unlimited simultaneous connections, but the antivirus app install limit is 5 devices. ESET plans range from 1 to 10 devices depending on which tier and configuration you choose.

### Does ESET have a free trial?

Yes. ESET offers a 30-day free trial on its HOME Security products, which is a practical way to evaluate the software before committing. Surfshark offers a 30-day money-back option rather than a pre-purchase trial — you subscribe, evaluate, and can request a refund within 30 days if it does not fit your needs.

### Which has better macOS support?

Surfshark Antivirus supports macOS 11 (Big Sur) and later. ESET HOME Security requires macOS 13 or later. If you are running macOS 11 or 12 and want active antivirus support, Surfshark covers those versions while the current ESET release does not.

### Is ESET a Slovak company? Does that affect privacy?

ESET is headquartered in Bratislava, Slovakia — an EU member state. Slovakia is subject to EU data protection law (GDPR) but sits outside the Fourteen Eyes intelligence-sharing network. For most consumer use cases, this distinction does not affect day-to-day privacy. For users with elevated concerns about intelligence-sharing jurisdictions, ESET's Slovak base is a mild plus relative to Dutch-registered Surfshark.
