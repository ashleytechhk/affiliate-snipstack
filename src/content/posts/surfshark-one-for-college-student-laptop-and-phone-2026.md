## Antivirus Performance: Does It Actually Protect You?

Independent testing labs like AV-TEST and AV-Comparatives rate Norton near the top of antivirus detection benchmarks. If you are studying cybersecurity or working with sensitive research data, the marginal antivirus edge in Norton may be worth the trade-off. For everyone else, the difference is noise.
