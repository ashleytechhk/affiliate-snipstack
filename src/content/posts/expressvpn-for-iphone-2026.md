The biggest driver is subscription arbitrage. Streaming services — entertainment platforms, music services, sports packages — set regional prices that can differ by 40–70% compared to higher-cost markets. Beyond price arbitrage, iPhone users also use VPNs to:

- Access content libraries that differ by territory
- Secure traffic on public Wi-Fi (airports, cafes, hotels)
- Keep browsing private from ISP-level data collection

Both ExpressVPN and Surfshark handle all three use cases. The question is which one does it better on iOS in 2026 — and at what cost.
