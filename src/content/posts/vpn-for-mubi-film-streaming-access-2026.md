## How a VPN Works for MUBI Access

A VPN — Virtual Private Network — creates an encrypted connection between your device and a server in a location of your choice. Once connected, websites and streaming platforms see the server's IP address rather than your actual location.

For MUBI, the mechanism works like this:

1. **You open your VPN app** and select a server in your target country — say, France.
2. **Your traffic routes through that server.** MUBI reads the French server's IP address and serves you France's catalogue.

The process works the same in reverse if you're travelling: connect to a server in your home country, and MUBI shows you your home library regardless of where you physically are.
