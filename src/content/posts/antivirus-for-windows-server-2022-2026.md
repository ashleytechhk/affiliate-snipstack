## The Real Risks Sitting on Your Server Right Now

Let us be specific about what can go wrong when a Windows Server 2022 machine runs without proper antivirus protection.

**Ransomware** is the most devastating threat. This is software that sneaks onto your server, quietly encrypts every file so you cannot open them, then demands a payment — usually hundreds to thousands of dollars in cryptocurrency — to unlock them. There is no guarantee paying will work. Many victims pay and still lose their data.

**Spyware** sits silently on your machine and watches everything: keystrokes, passwords, banking logins, emails. You would never know it was there until your bank account is drained.

**Remote Access Trojans (RATs)** give criminals complete control of your server. They can delete files, steal data, use your machine to attack others, and even watch through connected cameras.

**Phishing-triggered malware** arrives through email or a misleading website link. One wrong click on a message that looks like it is from your bank or the government, and malicious software installs itself automatically.
