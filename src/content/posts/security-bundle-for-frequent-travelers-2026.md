## The Traveler Problems Neither Norton Nor McAfee Actually Solve

![Security bundle for frequent travelers — traveler threat scenarios on hotel WiFi and airport networks](/og/security-bundle-for-frequent-travelers-2026-inline-1.png)

### Hotel and Airport WiFi Is a Real Attack Surface

Airport lounges and hotel networks are shared environments. On an unencrypted connection, someone with the right tools on the same network can intercept traffic that is not already protected by HTTPS. Most sites use HTTPS now, but not every app does — and the VPN in your Norton bundle caps out at 50GB per month.

For a traveler doing video calls, syncing cloud storage, and streaming in the evenings, 50GB disappears in days. Once it hits the cap, you are exposed on every unprotected network until your billing cycle resets.

[Surfshark One](https://snipstack.io/surfshark-one-vs-individual-apps-cost-2026/) Bundle's VPN has no data cap. Connect at the airport, at the hotel, in the conference center — the tunnel stays open.

### Your Banking App Sees a Foreign IP and Panics

Landing in Tokyo and opening your banking app from a Japanese IP is the kind of thing that triggers fraud flags, temporary account locks, and verification nightmares. A VPN lets you connect through a server in your home country before opening the banking app — your bank sees the same IP range it always does.

Norton's VPN can do this too, if you have not hit your monthly data cap. If you have, you are working without the tool.

### Scheduled Scans Do Not Care About Your Timezone

If Norton is set to scan Tuesday at 9 AM (the default for millions of users), and you are in Singapore where that is 9 AM Wednesday local time and you are mid-presentation, you know [what happens](https://snipstack.io/what-happens-when-email-is-in-data-breach-2026/): the laptop fan spins up, the CPU pegs at 80%, and the slide transitions start stuttering.

Surfshark's antivirus runs lighter. The initial scan is thorough; after that, it monitors new and changed files in real time without the scheduled full-disk CPU assault.

### The Renewal Email Arrives When You Are in a Different Country

Norton's introductory pricing is designed to feel reasonable — until year two, when the bill jumps from $39.99 to $109.99 and you are in a hotel room in Amsterdam with a weak connection and no time to deal with a cancellation chat queue. [Surfshark One](https://snipstack.io/surfshark-one-trial-available-2026/) Bundle's renewal pricing is more predictable, and the unlimited-device coverage means you are not paying extra as you add a travel router or a second laptop.
