What varies is:

## FAQ

**Is there any [email alias](https://snipstack.io/email-alias-for-twitch-account-privacy-2026/) service that offers custom domain support completely free?**

No — as of 2026, every major alias service (Alt-ID, SimpleLogin, addy.io, Firefox Relay, Proton Pass) requires a paid plan for custom domain support. The cheapest path to a custom domain alias is addy.io at $1/month. If you see claims of "free custom domain email alias," verify carefully — they typically mean a shared domain at no cost, not a user-supplied domain.

**What is the difference between a shared domain alias and a custom domain alias?**

A shared domain alias uses a domain controlled by the service provider — like `@altid.surfshark.net` or `@anonaddy.me`. A custom domain alias uses a domain you own and control — like `@yourdomain.com`. For stopping spam, both work identically. Custom domain matters if you want the alias to look professional or branded, or if you want to maintain control over the domain independent of the service provider.

**Does Alt-ID's alias domain get blocked by websites?**

Some websites attempt to block known alias domains. This is a known limitation across all alias services, not specific to Alt-ID. When a service blocks your alias, you have two options: use a different alias domain (if your service offers multiple), or use your real address for that specific account and manage it separately. [Alt-ID handles most mainstream signups without issue](/blog/altid?sub=email-alias-with-custom-domain-free-2026__faq).

**Can I keep using my aliases if I cancel my Surfshark subscription?**

Alt-ID aliases are tied to your Surfshark account. If you cancel your subscription, access to alias management — including creating new aliases and viewing existing ones — would be affected. Check Surfshark's current terms for the exact post-cancellation behavior. For indefinite alias control independent of a subscription, a standalone service like addy.io gives you more continuity options.

**Which service is best if I already have a VPN and just want a standalone alias tool?**

If you're not adding a VPN subscription, addy.io is the strongest free standalone option — unlimited standard aliases at no cost, with custom domain available for $1/month if you need it. SimpleLogin is the better choice if you want custom domain support with a more polished interface and don't mind $36/year.

**Will email aliases stop all spam?**

Aliases prevent new spam from reaching your real address for any service you sign up for going forward. They don't retroactively fix your existing real address if it's already on spam lists. For a full solution: use aliases for all future signups, and manage existing spam on your real address with inbox filters in parallel.
