## How to Cancel Norton: Step-by-Step

Before switching, you need to cancel cleanly to avoid another charge. Here is the process:

### Option 1: Cancel Through Your Norton Account (Recommended)

1. Go to **my.norton.com** and sign in.
2. Click your profile name in the top-right corner and select **My Subscriptions**.
3. Locate the active plan you want to cancel and click **Cancel Subscription** (sometimes labeled **Turn Off Auto-Renewal**).
4. Work through the confirmation screens — Norton will ask you to confirm at least twice.
5. Save the cancellation confirmation email Norton sends you.

Your access continues until the current billing period ends. You are not cut off immediately.

### Option 2: Cancel by Phone

Norton's support line is listed in your account dashboard under Contact Support. Phone cancellation can be faster if you run into issues with the online flow. Have your account email and billing details ready before you call.

### Option 3: Contact Your Bank

If you are past the refund window or cannot access your account, you can ask your bank to block future charges from Norton. This does not remove your account from Norton's system, but it stops the billing. Use this as a fallback only.

**After cancelling:** Uninstall Norton from every device. Background Norton services can continue running even after your subscription ends, continuing to slow down your system.
