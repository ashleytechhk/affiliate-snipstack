### Antivirus: Lightweight and Real-Time

[Surfshark Antivirus](https://snipstack.io/surfshark-antivirus-vs-bitdefender-speed-test-2026/) runs real-time protection on Windows and Android. On macOS, it operates in scheduled scan mode. It does not install a browser toolbar. It does not add a tray icon that pops up every time you launch a game. The scan engine is updated regularly and covers ransomware, trojans, and spyware categories.

### Alert: Breach Monitoring That Actually Notifies You

Surfshark Alert monitors your email addresses, credit card numbers, and passwords against known breach databases. When a match appears, you receive a notification with context — which breach, what data type, and what action to take. This is similar to what Norton LifeLock offers as a paid upgrade, except Alert is included in the base One subscription.

### Search: Private by Default

Surfshark Search pulls real results without logging your queries or building an ad profile around your history. It is not a replacement for Google if you rely heavily on local results or Maps integration, but for everyday informational searches it removes the surveillance layer entirely.
