## Why RaiPlay Is Geo-Locked to Italy

Geo-blocking exists because of content licensing, not arbitrary restriction. RAI acquires broadcast and streaming rights on a country-by-country basis — a film or co-produced drama often carries rights that only cover the Italian market, with international rights belonging to a different distributor. Streaming everything globally without restriction would undercut those separate licensing deals.

The enforcement is simple: every time you open RaiPlay, its servers check your IP address against a geolocation database. An Italian IP passes; anything else triggers the block, regardless of whether the account itself is genuinely Italian.

Like most major broadcasters, RAI also maintains blocklists of known VPN server IP ranges, so not every VPN works, or keeps working. The providers worth paying for actively rotate their Italian IPs to stay ahead.
