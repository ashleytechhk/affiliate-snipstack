## Why a Remote Work Household Is a Different Problem

A single remote worker's security needs are simple: one laptop, one VPN connection, done. A household is a different math problem entirely. You've got overlapping schedules (someone's always on a call), overlapping devices (work laptop, personal phone, kids' tablets, a shared smart TV), and one shared network that all of it runs through.

That means the privacy bundle you pick needs to handle simultaneous connections without a device cap, protect a mix of work and personal devices without slowing any of them down mid-meeting, and price itself in a way that doesn't punish you for having a family. Most legacy antivirus suites were built around the "one person, one PC" model from a decade ago. Households in 2026 have outgrown that model, and the pricing hasn't always caught up — except in the newer bundles.
