## Troubleshooting: Common Questions

### "My Mac says the app is from an unidentified developer"

This is a standard Mac security message — it does not mean anything is wrong. Here is [what to do](https://snipstack.io/what-to-do-after-malware-infection-2026/):

1. Open **System Settings** (or System Preferences on older Macs).
2. Click **Privacy & Security**.
3. Scroll down and look for a message about Surfshark being blocked.
4. Click **Open Anyway**.

### "The scan seems to be taking forever"

If your Mac has a lot of files (photos, documents, videos), the first scan can take longer. Let it run overnight if needed. Future scans will be faster.
