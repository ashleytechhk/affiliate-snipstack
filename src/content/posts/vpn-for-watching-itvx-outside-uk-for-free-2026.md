## Why ITVX Only Works Inside the UK

The restriction comes from rights licensing, not an arbitrary rule. ITV buys and sells broadcast rights territory by territory — a commissioned drama might have separate international distribution rights sold elsewhere. Opening ITVX globally would undercut those deals.

Enforcement is an IP-to-location check on every request: a UK address passes, anything else gets the geo-block. Like most broadcasters, ITVX also maintains a blocklist of known VPN ranges, so a VPN needs a UK server pool large enough — and rotated often enough — to stay ahead of it.
