## The Four Threats Your Old Laptop Faces Right Now

![The biggest threats facing older Windows laptops in 2026 — illustrated](/og/lightweight-antivirus-for-older-windows-laptop-2026-inline-1.png)

Understanding what you are protecting against makes it easier to see why the software matters. Here are the four threats most likely to reach an older Windows laptop in 2026.

### Ransomware

Ransomware is a type of virus that locks every file on your computer — photos, documents, everything — and demands payment to unlock them. Older Windows versions are disproportionately targeted because known security vulnerabilities have been public knowledge for years. Once ransomware runs, recovering files without paying is extremely difficult. Prevention, via a real-time antivirus, is the only reliable defence.

### Tech-Support Scam Pop-Ups

You are browsing a recipe website or reading the news, and suddenly a large pop-up fills the screen: "WARNING: Your computer has been infected. Call Microsoft Support now: 1-800-XXX-XXXX." The [phone number](https://snipstack.io/phone-number-for-facebook-verification-2026/) connects to a scammer, not Microsoft. They will ask for remote access to your computer and your credit card number. A good antivirus blocks the websites that trigger these pop-ups before they ever load.

### Phishing Emails

These are emails that look exactly like they came from your bank, from Medicare, from Amazon, or even from a family member. They contain a link that takes you to a [fake website](https://snipstack.io/fake-website-detector-tool-2026/) designed to steal your password or card number. Surfshark's real-time web protection checks every link before you land on the page and warns you if it is dangerous.

### Spyware

Spyware runs silently in the background, recording everything you type: passwords, bank account numbers, [Social Security](https://snipstack.io/social-security-scam-call-verify-2026/) numbers. You would never know it was there without a scanner actively looking for it. Real-time antivirus catches spyware during installation, before it has a chance to record anything.
