## Why Free Trials Are Especially Dangerous

Dating apps love free trials. So do scammers.

A 30-day premium trial on a popular dating platform often requires phone verification. You type in your number, get the SMS code, and activate the trial. Done. But what you may not realize is that the number you used is now associated with your profile and stored in that company's database. Marketing systems tag these numbers as "high churn risk" and blast them with re-engagement messages. Third-party retargeting firms buy that behavior data. Your number graduates from a dating app's CRM to a dozen marketing lists before your trial week is even over.
