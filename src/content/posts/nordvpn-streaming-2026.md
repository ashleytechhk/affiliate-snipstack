## Speed: Does the Gap Actually Matter for Streaming?

NordVPN's NordLynx protocol (built on WireGuard) consistently benchmarks faster than Surfshark's WireGuard implementation in independent speed tests — often 10–15% higher on a single connection. In absolute numbers, both services exceed 300 Mbps on a standard fiber connection by a wide margin.

Netflix 4K requires 25 Mbps. Disney+ 4K requires 25 Mbps. Even accounting for VPN overhead, a 300 Mbps connection leaves enormous headroom. The speed difference between NordVPN and Surfshark is real in benchmark labs; it is essentially invisible in a living room.

Where NordVPN's speed advantage becomes meaningful is large file downloads, gaming with latency sensitivity, or video calls routed through a VPN. For pure streaming arbitrage use — changing region to watch a show — the gap is irrelevant.
