## Why France TV Is Geo-Blocked Outside France

The block is contractual, not accidental. France Télévisions licenses much of its content territory by territory — a foreign drama, a sports broadcast, or a co-produced documentary often comes with rights covering France only. Opening the platform globally would breach those agreements. So every connection runs through an IP-location check: a French IP passes, anything else gets the geo-block message, regardless of how valid the account behind it is.

The fix has to look like a French IP address. And like most national broadcasters, france.tv also maintains blocklists targeting known VPN server ranges — so the VPN you choose needs to rotate its IP pool fast enough to stay ahead of them.
