## Troubleshooting

### "My dating app rejected the number during SMS verification"

Some apps use third-party verification systems that flag known VoIP ranges. Alt-Number uses real mobile number ranges rather than standard VoIP blocks, which significantly reduces rejection rates. If you do hit a rejection, try selecting a number from a different country or a different area code. Avoid reusing a number that was previously associated with another account on the same app.

### "I'm not receiving SMS messages"

First, check that notifications are enabled for the Surfshark app in your phone's settings. On iOS, go to **Settings > Notifications > Surfshark** and confirm notifications are on. On Android, check **Settings > Apps > Surfshark > Notifications**. If notifications are on and messages still aren't arriving, try toggling Alt-Number off and on in the app, or force-quit and reopen Surfshark.

Also confirm your subscription is active — if a payment failed, the number may be in a suspended state.

### "The number I want is unavailable"

Number availability varies by country and area code. If your preferred area code shows no available numbers, select a nearby region or a different country. For dating use, the specific area code matters less than having a real, working number — matches rarely pay close attention to the area code of a number they're texting.

### "Calls go to voicemail instead of ringing"

Check that Do Not Disturb is not active on your phone, and that the Surfshark app has background app refresh enabled. On iOS: **Settings > General > Background App Refresh > Surfshark** should be on. On Android: ensure battery optimization is not restricting Surfshark from running in the background.
