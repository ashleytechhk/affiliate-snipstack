If you use any of these apps with your real number, a new contact who adds your number instantly sees your profile photo, display name, and in WhatsApp's case, your status and last-seen timestamp. **Carrier metadata persistence** means that even if you block someone, the record of two numbers being in contact does not disappear from carrier logs. The connection is documented.

**Location narrowing from area code and context** is not precise, but it is meaningful. An area code narrows geography. Combined with your name from a reverse lookup and your workplace from LinkedIn, a motivated person can get within blocks.

None of this requires technical sophistication. The tools are free, public, and fast.
