## Troubleshooting

**I am not receiving forwarded emails.**
Check that forwarding is toggled on for that specific alias (Step 3). Also verify your real email address is typed correctly in the forwarding field — a single typo is the most common cause.

**The website rejected my Alt ID address.**
Some aggressive spam filters block lesser-known domains. Try generating a new alias — Alt ID rotates domains occasionally. If the site still rejects it, use the alias as a display name workaround or contact Surfshark support.

**I forgot which alias I used for a site.**
Open **My Aliases** in the dashboard. Each alias shows you when it was created and how many emails have been forwarded through it, which usually jogs your memory.

**My password was not saved.**
Alt ID's generator does not auto-save passwords — it is a generation tool, not a vault. Copy the password immediately and store it in your preferred password manager before navigating away.

**I want to stop all forwarding temporarily.**
You can pause individual aliases or toggle forwarding off globally from the account settings page. Your aliases remain intact; you just will not receive any mail until you switch forwarding back on.
