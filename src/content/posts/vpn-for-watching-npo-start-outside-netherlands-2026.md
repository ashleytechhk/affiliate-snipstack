## Why NPO Start Is Blocked Outside the Netherlands

NPO licenses its programming territory by territory. Content co-produced abroad, or carrying separate international distribution rights, comes with contractual limits on where it can stream. Every connection triggers an IP-location check: a Dutch IP passes, anything else gets the regional block.

Here's the part that matters for your wallet: free NPO Start is blocked everywhere outside the Netherlands. NPO Plus is different — EU cross-border portability rules keep it working across the EU/EEA (EU countries plus Norway, Liechtenstein, Iceland) without a VPN. Outside that zone — UK, US, Australia, anywhere else — Plus hits the identical block as the free tier. You'd be paying €3.49/month for a fix that stops working exactly when you need it most.
